## 1. Al-Anon WSO — meeting data

**To:** afgmobile@al-anon.org
**Subject:** Permission to include Al-Anon meeting listings in an open, noncommercial help-services directory

Hello,

I'm building SUCCURRO, an open, noncommercial dataset and website of local help
and support services across the US — food banks, shelters, treatment programs,
and mutual-aid meetings — so that someone can enter their town and what they
need and find current, verified help nearby.

I'd like to include Al-Anon and Alateen meeting listings: meeting name,
schedule, venue address or online format, and language — the same facts your
public meeting search shows. Nothing about members, and no contact information
beyond what Al-Anon publishes.

I'm writing before using any data because I couldn't find published reuse
terms. Specifically, may I (a) periodically retrieve the meeting dataset that
powers your website's meeting search, and (b) republish those meeting facts in
an openly licensed dataset, with every record attributed and linked back to
al-anon.org? If there's a preferred data channel or conditions you'd want
(update frequency, required wording, exclusions), I'll follow them exactly,
and I'll honor any removal request without argument.

Happy to answer any questions about the project.

Thank you,
Kara Zajac

**Status:** sent 2026-07-19

## 2. AmpleHarvest.org — pantry registry

**To:** (contact address on ampleharvest.org — FAQ directs email inquiries)
**Subject:** Data partnership: including AmpleHarvest pantries in an open help-services directory

Hello,

I'm building SUCCURRO, an open, noncommercial dataset and website of local
help and support services across the US, resolved to the city level — food
assistance, shelters, treatment, recovery meetings, and more. The food layer
currently draws on USDA, Feeding America's member list, and other public
sources, and your registry of pantries that opted in to be found is the best
pantry-level coverage in the country.

Rather than scrape your site, I'd like to ask directly: would AmpleHarvest
share a periodic export or feed of pantry listings (name, address, hours,
whatever you're comfortable sharing) for inclusion? Every record would be
attributed to AmpleHarvest and linked back to your pantry page — which should
send both donors and people seeking food to you — and I'll honor any
conditions or removal requests. The dataset is openly licensed and
noncommercial; if a narrower license on your slice matters to you, we can
accommodate that.

Thanks for considering it — and for what AmpleHarvest does.

Kara Zajac

**Status:** sent 2026-07-19

## 3. Immigration Advocates Network — legal services directory

**To:** directory@immigrationadvocates.org
**Subject:** Partnership request: National Immigration Legal Services Directory in an open help-services dataset

Hello,

I'm building SUCCURRO, an open, noncommercial dataset and website of local
help and support services across the US — the goal is that someone can enter
their town and what they need (food, shelter, treatment, legal help) and find
current, verified services nearby. Legal aid is one of the weakest-covered
areas in public data, and your National Immigration Legal Services Directory
is the authoritative source for nonprofit immigration legal providers.

I know you already syndicate the directory to partner organizations, so I'm
asking through the front door: could SUCCURRO include directory records
(organization, office locations, services, languages, populations served),
with attribution and a link back on every record? I'm glad to work within
whatever terms you use for partners — update cadence, required wording,
exclusions for records that shouldn't be republished — and I'll honor removal
requests immediately. Given who this directory serves, I care a great deal
about only ever showing current, accurate information; a stewarded feed from
you beats any snapshot.

Happy to share more about the project or discuss over a call.

Thank you,
Kara Zajac

**Status:** sent 2026-07-19
